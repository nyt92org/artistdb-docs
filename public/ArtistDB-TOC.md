# ArtistDB Terms of Use

ArtistDB is a database full of SFW/NSFW artists that are free and open-source platform for artists to showcase their work and connect with potential followers and collectors. Our platform offers additional features for artists to gain visibility and build their brand.
ArtistDB respects the copyright of each artist's work and requires artists to provide explicit permission and source for their work to be used on ArtistDB unless the art is under a specific license or fair use and public domain.

- ArtistDB tolorates:

  - Fair use
  - For Personal, developer, educational, non-commercial and commercial use
  - In a manner that does not infringe the rights of others
  - For the purpose of promoting the artist's work
  - 

- ArtistDB does not tolerate:

  - Harassment, Spam, Impersonation, Illegal activity
  - Any other activity that violates the rights of others
  - Abusing the API usage and selling the API to others
  - AI and scraping our website and API in a high volume

- For Commercial Purposes: The usage of API is allowed for commercial but in a specific conditions:

  - It is used for only specific purposes (does not apply during testing and development)
  - Must be approved by the ArtistDB team if the usage is higher than our global limit
  - The company must contribute to the ArtistDB project (1-8%) if the company made at least $50k-100k per year
  - Does not violate the license
  - Does not violate our terms and conditions
  - Does not violate the rights of artists
  - Does not use our API for illegal purposes, AI and scraping our website and API in a high volume
  - Abusing the API usage and selling the API to others
